🛡 BACK BET CALCULATOR
Dual-bet recovery system · Recovery Bet chases profit · Back Bet protects your money
✓
RECOVERY BET WINS
Covers all previous round losses + both current bets + your target profit. Full recovery with guaranteed profit.
↔
BACK BET WINS
Returns exactly the total staked in this round only (Recovery + Back Bet). No profit, but no cumulative loss either.
✗
BOTH LOSE
Both bets lost. Move to the next round — the new Recovery Bet is already sized to recover everything including this round.

🎲 ARBITRARY CALCULATOR
Multi-odd recovery system · Bet on 2–6 odds simultaneously · Equal profit whichever odd wins
🎯
ANY ODD WINS
Whichever of your selected odds wins, the payout is sized to return all previous losses plus your exact target profit.
⚖
EQUAL PROFIT
Each odd gets a different stake — higher odds need less money, lower odds need more — but all deliver the same profit.
🔄
ALL LOSE → NEXT ROUND
If every odd loses, the next round's bets are recalculated to recover the full combined loss plus profit.
