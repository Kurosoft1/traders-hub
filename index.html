HOW TO USE KUROSOFT CASINO
Complete guide to every tool and feature
📊 Odds Calculator
Convert between all major odds formats: Decimal, Fractional, American (Moneyline), and Implied Probability. Enter any format and instantly see all others. Use this to compare odds from different bookmakers that display in different formats.
🎯 Probability Calculator
Calculate combined probabilities for accumulators and parlays. Enter individual event probabilities and see the combined chance of all events occurring together. Helps you understand the real probability behind multi-bet accumulators.
💰 Stake Manager (Kelly Criterion)
Calculates the mathematically optimal stake size using the Kelly Criterion formula. Enter your bankroll, the odds, and your estimated probability of winning. The calculator tells you exactly how much to stake to maximize long-term growth while minimizing risk of ruin.
🔄 Martingale Calculator
Plan your entire Martingale betting sequence before you start. Enter your base stake, odds, and number of rounds. See the exact stake amount for each round, cumulative exposure, and the bankroll needed. Know your limits before committing — this tool shows you when the math stops working.
🛡️ Back-Bet Recovery System
A dual-bet system with two components: the Recovery Bet (chases profit at higher odds) and the Back Bet (protects your money at lower odds). Enter your target profit, select your odds, and the calculator shows exactly how much to place on each bet. If the high-odds bet wins, you profit. If it loses, the back bet recovers your money.
⚖️ Arbitrary Calculator (Multi-Odd)
Bet on 2 to 6 different odds simultaneously and guarantee equal profit regardless of which one wins. Enter your total stake and the odds for each selection. The calculator distributes stakes so that no matter which bet wins, your payout is the same. Perfect for hedging across multiple outcomes.
💹 Arbitrage Scanner
Scans live odds from 40+ bookmakers across Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Champions League, and NBA. Automatically detects when different bookmakers offer odds that create a guaranteed profit opportunity (arbitrage). Shows you exactly which bookmaker to bet with, how much to stake, and your guaranteed profit percentage.
🏀 NBA & ⚽ Football Predictions
AI-generated predictions for upcoming matches. Each prediction shows win probabilities for home/away/draw, a confidence rating, and recommended picks. For football: match result (1X2) and Both Teams to Score (GG/NG). For NBA: moneyline picks and over/under analysis. Save your picks to the Prediction History page to track your win/loss record over time.
📋 Prediction History
Track every prediction you have saved. After matches are played, real scores are fetched automatically to determine if your pick was a Win or Loss. View your overall hit rate, win streaks, and performance by sport. This is your personal track record — use it to refine your strategy over time.
🎰 Lottery Lab
AI-powered lottery analysis covering UK 49s, Ghana Lotto, US Powerball, Mega Millions, EuroMillions, China Welfare, SA Powerball, Nigeria Lotto, and more. Uses 6 statistical models (frequency analysis, Bayesian probability, Markov chains, gap analysis, clustering, and regression) that learn and adapt over time. View number statistics, hot/cold numbers, and generate AI predictions.
🔴 49ja Live
Real-time feed of Bet9ja 49 Balls lottery draws. Connects to the live game API to show results as they happen, with colour analysis, frequency tracking, and pattern detection. The Stats page provides deep analysis of historical draws with number frequency, gap analysis, and due-number predictions.
✈️ Aviator Analytics
Connects to a live Aviator game scraper to pull real multiplier results. Displays distribution charts, streak analysis, and cash-out simulations. Enter your VPS scraper URL to connect, or manually input multipliers. Use the statistics to understand the mathematical patterns behind crash game outcomes.
▷ Strategy Backtester
Test any staking strategy against historical data before risking real money. Choose a system (flat stake, percentage, Martingale, Fibonacci), set your bankroll, and simulate hundreds of bets. See your projected P&L curve, maximum drawdown, win rate, and ROI. Know whether a strategy works before you use it.
▷ Data Collection
Log every bet you place with full details: sport, event, market, odds, stake, and result. Build your personal betting dataset over time. Use this data to identify which sports, leagues, and bet types give you the best returns — and which ones to avoid.
